Cardozo Engineering, Inc. (CE) is a Hispanic-owned civil and environmental engineering firm founded in 1999. CE's area of expertise is in providing engineering planning, design and construction management services on municipal water, sewer, and stormwater transmission and treatment facilities. CE staff has prepared master plans, conducted CMOM studies, managed SSES programs and designed and managed the construction of pipelines, pump stations and treatment facilities ranging from under $100K up to $100 million. We currently serve municipal clients in Florida.
